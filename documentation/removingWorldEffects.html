Removing World Effects

World effects require textures, and so the easiest way to hide world effects is to modify their textures to be blank. For example, the below texture is used for the lines on World 2 (In the volcano).

Texture 6 within /bg/bg_lav2.tpl

This texture can be replaced with a pure black one to hide the World 2 lines effect. A similar process can be applied on different world effects, but on different textures. Textures can be found in /bg/*.tpl, where *.tpl means any TPL file.
